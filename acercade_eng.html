We are a Costa Rican company with national capital specialized in exporting wood native to the Central American region, primarily Teak (Tectonis Grandis), Gmelina (Gmelina Arborea), and Cocobolo (Dalbergia Retusa)

Our focus markets are the Republic of India and the People's Republic of China. However, we are in the process of expanding into European and North American markets.

Our company has the capital and experience necessary to manage the entire process related to exporting wood. We begin the process by extracting the trees from their native soil. Next, we measure and cut the product to the specifications dictated by the customer. Lastly, we coordinate all the logistics required to place the final product in the shipping port dictated by the customer. By controlling the entire process, our company is able to guarantee complete satisfaction to our customers.

Cote-K is fully registered and licensed by the Government of Costa Rica, which represents an assurance to our customers and commercial partners that our operations adhere to international standards and laws. Similarly, our company is motivated by an enduring commitment to conduct transparent and reliable business, seeking to create everlasting bonds with each and everyone of our customers.

Mission

Offer high quality products and services to satisfy our customers and create long term business relationships.

Vision

Become the furthest reaching wood export company in the region, offering a full range of services to our customers, while at the same time guaranteeing total satisfaction.
