Costa Rica is a country located in Central America, in the tropical region, whose climate allows for a large variety of natural beauties, flora, and fauna.

These natural conditions result in an excellent habitat for a wide range of tree species, creating high quality wood ready for export.

Cote-K is a company dedicated to the exportation of logs and timber of species such as Teak (Tectonis Grandis), Gmelina (Gmelina Arborea), Cocobolo (Dalbergia Retusa), and others, to the Republic of India and The People’s Republic of China.


Our company is currently expanding commercial operations into countries in Europe and North America, adjusting to the demands of each market. We welcome you to contact us to discuss how our company can fulfill your requirements.
